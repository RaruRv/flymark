United Kingdom (UK)

The United Kingdom, comprising England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland, is a treasure trove of history, culture, and picturesque landscapes. From the historic castles of Scotland to the bustling streets of London, the UK offers a blend of tradition and modernity.

Key Destinations:

London: The capital city is a global hub known for its iconic landmarks such as the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum.

Edinburgh: A city steeped in history, Edinburgh boasts the medieval Edinburgh Castle, the Royal Mile, and the annual Edinburgh Festival.

Lake District: A serene region of lakes and mountains, the Lake District is a haven for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Cultural and Culinary Delights:

Explore the rich literary heritage of England with visits to Shakespeare's birthplace in Stratford-upon-Avon or the Brontë Parsonage Museum in Haworth. Indulge in traditional afternoon tea or savor classic fish and chips.

Outdoor Adventures:

Discover the wild beauty of the Scottish Highlands, hike the trails of Snowdonia in Wales, or stroll through the charming gardens of the English countryside.

Practical Tips:

Currency: British Pound Sterling (GBP)
