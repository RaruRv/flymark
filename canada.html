Canada

Canada, the second-largest country in the world, is known for its stunning natural beauty, friendly locals, and multicultural cities. From the picturesque Rocky Mountains to the vibrant city life of Toronto, Canada offers a balanced mix of outdoor adventures and urban exploration.

Key Destinations:

Banff National Park: Nestled in the heart of the Rockies, Banff National Park is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering hiking, skiing, and breathtaking views of turquoise lakes.

Vancouver: A coastal city surrounded by mountains and water, Vancouver is known for its diverse culinary scene, outdoor activities, and cultural events.

Quebec City: Immerse yourself in the charming cobblestone streets and French-inspired architecture of this historic city, which transports visitors to Old World Europe.

Cultural and Culinary Delights:

Canada's cultural diversity is reflected in its cuisine. Try poutine in Quebec, fresh seafood in Vancouver, or experience the multicultural flavors of Toronto's ethnic neighborhoods.

Outdoor Adventures:

Canada is a paradise for nature lovers. Explore the rugged coastline of Newfoundland, witness the Northern Lights in the Yukon, or kayak through the pristine waters of Lake Louise in Alberta.

Practical Tips:

Currency: Canadian Dollar (CAD)

Language: English and French (in Quebec)
