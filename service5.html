Permanent Visa:


Flymark's Permanent Visa service is tailored for individuals seeking to make a new country their permanent home. Whether you're pursuing career opportunities, family reunification, or a change in lifestyle, our experts guide you through the complex process of obtaining permanent residency in your desired destination.

Features:

Destination Expertise: Benefit from our in-depth knowledge of permanent residency requirements for various countries, ensuring accurate and up-to-date guidance.

Customized Consultation: Receive personalized consultation to understand the specific eligibility criteria, application process, and documentation requirements for your chosen destination.

Application Support: Our experts provide comprehensive assistance in preparing and submitting your permanent residency application, ensuring compliance with immigration regulations.

Application Process:

Consultation: Engage in an in-depth consultation with our immigration experts to assess your eligibility and discuss the permanent residency options available.

Eligibility Assessment: Receive a thorough assessment of your eligibility for permanent residency, considering factors such as education, work experience, and language proficiency.

Document Preparation: Our team assists in the preparation of all required documents, including educational certificates, work references, and proof of funds.

Application Submission: Flymark submits your permanent residency application to the relevant immigration authorities, ensuring adherence to timelines and guidelines.

Additional Services:

Pre-Departure Orientation: Receive guidance on relocating to your new country, including information on housing, healthcare, and cultural integration.

Post-Arrival Support: Benefit from post-arrival assistance, helping you settle into your new life, including assistance with social security numbers, healthcare enrollment, and more.

Benefits of Permanent Visa Service:

Global Opportunities: Unlock opportunities for career growth, education, and a higher quality of life by obtaining permanent residency in a new country.

Expert Guidance: Navigate the complexities of permanent residency applications with the support of experienced immigration consultants.

Smooth Transition: Flymark ensures a smooth transition to your new country, providing support from the application stage to post-arrival settlement.
