Israel

Israel, a land with deep historical significance, offers a blend of ancient sites, diverse landscapes, and a vibrant modern culture. From the ancient city of Jerusalem to the cosmopolitan atmosphere of Tel Aviv, Israel is a country of contrasts.

Key Destinations:

Jerusalem: A city sacred to three major religions, Jerusalem is home to the Western Wall, the Church of the Holy Sepulchre, and the Dome of the Rock.

Tel Aviv: Known for its modern architecture, lively beaches, and thriving nightlife, Tel Aviv is a hub of contemporary culture.

Dead Sea: The lowest point on Earth, the Dead Sea offers a unique experience of floating effortlessly due to its high salt concentration.

Cultural and Culinary Delights:

Explore the ancient streets of Jaffa, indulge in traditional Middle Eastern cuisine, and experience the vibrant arts and music scene.

Outdoor Adventures:

Hike the trails of Masada, relax by the shores of the Red Sea, or explore the diverse landscapes of the Negev Desert.

Practical Tips:

Currency: Israeli New Shekel (ILS)

Language: Hebrew
