Japan


Japan, a country where tradition meets innovation, is known for its cherry blossoms, historic temples, and cutting-edge technology. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene landscapes of Kyoto, Japan offers a journey through time.

Key Destinations:

Tokyo: The capital city is a dynamic metropolis with futuristic skyscrapers, traditional markets, and vibrant districts like Shibuya.

Kyoto: Steeped in history, Kyoto is home to beautiful temples, traditional tea houses, and the iconic Fushimi Inari Shrine.

Hiroshima: A city with a poignant history, Hiroshima is a symbol of peace and reconciliation, featuring the Peace Memorial Park.

Cultural and Culinary Delights:

Experience traditional tea ceremonies, witness the art of Geisha in Gion, and savor sushi, ramen, and other Japanese culinary delights.

Outdoor Adventures:

Explore the scenic landscapes of Hakone, bathe in hot springs, or hike the Nakasendo Trail for a blend of nature and culture.

Practical Tips:

Currency: Japanese Yen (JPY)

Language: Japanese
